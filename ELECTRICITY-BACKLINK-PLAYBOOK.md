# Electricity Backlink Playbook (India)

Use this after deployment to build links for the electricity topic cluster.

## Objective

- Make CalcVerse the strongest reference for electricity bill queries in India.
- Build links to the electricity hub and cluster pages, not just the homepage.
- Increase topical authority for electricity bill, unit-rate, slab, and tariff keywords.
- Protect and scale existing click momentum on JUSCO pages by prioritizing JUSCO-focused outreach angles.

## JUSCO Priority Note

- JUSCO already has strong click demand, so keep it as the first deep-page pitch in outreach.
- In each outreach batch, include at least one JUSCO-led angle and one broader hub angle.
- Use JUSCO page as a proof point, then upsell editors toward linked slab and unit-rate explainers.

## Outreach Targets

- Personal finance blogs covering household budgeting.
- Energy and utility explainers (state/city websites, local information portals).
- Education and exam prep sites with practical math/utility articles.
- Housing/rental blogs discussing monthly living costs in India.
- Local news explainers writing seasonal electricity stories (summer bill spikes).

## Anchor Strategy

Use natural anchors only. Mix brand, partial match, and URL anchors.

- Brand anchors:
  - CalcVerse electricity calculators
  - CalcVerse electricity bill tool
- Partial match anchors:
  - electricity bill calculator India
  - 1 unit electricity cost in India
  - electricity slab rate explained
  - JUSCO bill calculator
  - JUSCO electricity rate per unit
  - Jamshedpur electricity bill calculator
- Neutral anchors:
  - this bill estimator
  - this guide
  - source
