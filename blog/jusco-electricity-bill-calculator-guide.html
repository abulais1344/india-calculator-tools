JUSCO Electricity Bill Calculator Guide

Use this quick guide to estimate your JUSCO monthly bill and avoid billing surprises.

How to Estimate JUSCO Bill

  1. Take units consumed from your latest bill.
  2. Enter your effective per-unit tariff.
  3. Add fixed charge and any known surcharge.
  4. Use a calculator to compare low, normal, and summer usage.

Try the calculator: Electricity Bill Calculator India.

Example

If monthly usage is 320 units, tariff is Rs 7.40, and fixed charge is Rs 140:

Total estimate = (320 x 7.40) + 140 = Rs 2,508

Tips to Reduce Bill

  • Track AC and geyser hours weekly.
  • Replace old fans and lights with efficient models.
  • Compare month-on-month units, not just final payable amount.
