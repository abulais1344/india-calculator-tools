Frequently Asked Questions

What is the NEET 2026 marking scheme?

Each correct answer earns +4 marks. Each wrong answer deducts 1 mark. Unattempted questions score 0. Maximum possible score is 720 (45+45+90 questions × 4).

What score is needed to clear NEET cutoff for general category?

For general category, the cutoff is typically 50th percentile. Based on recent years, this corresponds to roughly 360–400+ marks. However, actual cutoffs are decided post-exam by NTA.

What is the difference between NEET score and percentile?

Score is the raw marks (e.g. 540/720). Percentile is a relative rank showing how many candidates you outperformed out of the total who appeared. A 97 percentile with 540 marks means you scored higher than 97% of all candidates.

How many students appear for NEET each year?

Over 20 lakh (2 million) students typically appear for NEET. In 2024, approximately 24 lakh candidates registered. This number affects competitiveness significantly.
