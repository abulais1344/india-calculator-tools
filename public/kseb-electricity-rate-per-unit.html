KSEB Electricity Rate Per Unit

KSEB electricity rate per unit varies by slab, usage level, and billing structure. If you want a realistic Kerala bill estimate, match your monthly units to the provider calculator.

What to Check Before Estimating

  • Your expected monthly units for the full billing cycle
  • Whether the household is close to a higher slab threshold
  • Fixed charges and other additions beyond the energy rate
  • The difference between a low-usage month and a peak-usage month
