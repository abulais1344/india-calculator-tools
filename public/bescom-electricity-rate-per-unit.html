BESCOM Electricity Rate Per Unit

BESCOM electricity rate per unit is only one part of the final Karnataka bill. Slab changes, fixed charges, and usage pattern usually matter more than a single quoted rate.

What to Check Before Estimating the Bill

  • Your expected units for the billing cycle
  • Whether usage is likely to cross into a higher slab
  • Any fixed charge applied to the connection
  • The difference between energy charge and final bill total
