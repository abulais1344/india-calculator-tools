What Is the Price of 1 kWh Electricity in India?

The price of 1 kWh electricity in India is not one national fixed number. It varies by state electricity board, consumer category, slab, and fixed charges.

Why 1 Unit Cost Changes

  • Each state or provider has its own tariff rules
  • Residential and commercial rates differ
  • Higher slabs can increase average per unit cost
  • Fixed charges change the effective monthly rate

Use a Better Estimate

If you want a real answer, use your board name, units consumed, and fixed charge instead of assuming one flat national rate.
