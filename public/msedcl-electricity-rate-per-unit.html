MSEDCL Electricity Rate Per Unit

MSEDCL electricity rate per unit depends on slab, consumer category, and fixed charges. A realistic Maharashtra bill estimate needs your units consumed, not just a flat rate assumption.

What Changes the MSEDCL Rate

  • Domestic versus commercial category
  • Monthly slab crossing as units increase
  • Fixed charges and taxes added to the bill
  • Regional tariff updates in Maharashtra

Best Next Step

If you want to estimate the next MSEDCL bill accurately, use the provider calculator with your current month units and then compare it against a higher-usage month.
