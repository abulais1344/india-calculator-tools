TPDDL Electricity Rate Per Unit

TPDDL electricity rate per unit changes with domestic usage, slab crossing, and fixed charges. Use the Delhi-specific calculator if you want a practical bill estimate instead of a flat rate guess.

What Usually Changes the TPDDL Bill

  • Monthly units consumed and whether they cross a higher slab
  • Domestic versus commercial billing category
  • Fixed charges and taxes added to the energy charge
  • Seasonal usage spikes from cooling or heating needs

Best Next Step
