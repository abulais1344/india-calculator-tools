MP Poorv Kshetra Bill Calculator Guide

A practical way to estimate monthly electricity cost for MP Poorv Kshetra users.

Bill Estimate Formula

Total bill = (Units Consumed x Effective Unit Rate) + Fixed Charges + Other Applicable Charges

For planning, this formula is enough to forecast expected monthly cost before the final utility bill arrives.

Worked Example

Assume 410 units in a month, effective rate Rs 6.90, fixed charge Rs 165:

Estimate = (410 x 6.90) + 165 = Rs 2,994

How to Use This for Budgeting

  • Run three scenarios: low, normal, and peak summer usage.
  • Keep monthly unit target based on past 6 months average.
  • Review high-load appliances and reduce standby consumption.
