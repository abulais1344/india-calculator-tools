Per Unit Electricity Rate in India

Per unit electricity rate in India varies by state, DISCOM, consumer type, and slab. A useful estimate needs your provider and monthly usage, not just one flat national number.

What Changes the Per Unit Rate

  • Residential vs commercial connection
  • Monthly slab crossing
  • State electricity board tariffs
  • Fixed charges and fuel adjustments
