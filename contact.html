What We're Looking For

  • Calculation Errors: If results don't match expected values or contain bugs.
  • UI/UX Issues: Problems with navigation, design, or mobile responsiveness.
  • Feature Ideas: New calculators, tools, or calculator enhancements.
  • Accessibility Issues: Difficulties using tools on screen readers or with keyboard navigation.
  • Documentation Gaps: Missing or unclear explanations in calculator pages.
  • General Feedback: Your thoughts on the platform, what works, what doesn't.

Response Time

We typically respond to emails within 48 hours. For critical bug reports affecting calculator accuracy, we prioritize fixes and will reach out to confirm the issue and expected resolution timeline.

FAQ

I submitted feedback but haven't heard back. What should I do?

Please check your spam folder first. If you don't see our response within 48 hours, feel free to email again with a note that this is a followup. Sometimes emails get lost!

Can I request a feature that doesn't exist?

Absolutely! We love feature requests. While we can't guarantee implementation of every idea, we do evaluate all suggestions. Send us an email at features@calcverse.in with your idea.

Is there a phone number or chat support?

Currently, we support contact via email. This helps us manage inquiries efficiently and create a paper trail for feature discussion. We aim to be responsive via email rather than offer phone support.

Can my calculator idea be implemented on this platform?

If your idea is relevant to individuals or small businesses in India and aligns with our calculator/document generator focus, we'll definitely consider it. Tell us how it would help users and we'll evaluate it.
