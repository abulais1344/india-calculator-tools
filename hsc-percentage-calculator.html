Frequently Asked Questions

How is Maharashtra HSC percentage calculated?

Add up all marks obtained across subjects, divide by the total maximum marks, then multiply by 100. Both theory and practical marks are included.

Does Maharashtra HSC use best-of-5 rule?

No. Maharashtra Board considers all subjects for the aggregate percentage, unlike CBSE which applies a Best of 5 rule. Every subject mark counts.

What percentage is needed for Distinction in HSC?

You need 75% or above to earn Distinction in Maharashtra HSC exams.

Are practical marks included in HSC percentage?

Yes. For Science subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, practical marks are added to theory marks and the combined score counts toward your percentage.
